Who are we?

About MoonUp

MOONup are a group of astronomers based in Cork city, with a particular interest in the lunar landscape. We want to share our knowledge and give people a detailed view of our moon, a view they may not have experienced before.

Where?

We usually observe in Kennedy Park near Cork city center. Google map with more detail below. Tell your friends and family, the more the merrier. Dogs welcome!

Anything else?

We advise you to dress warmly. If possible bring a hot drink and some snacks. A collapsible camping chair will be useful. Bring binoculars if you can, they provide good views of the moon and allow you to keep observing when waiting for your turn on the scopes. Bring a DSLR if you have one and we can show you how to start astro-photography.
